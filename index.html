Ahmed Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Welcome to Ahmed Tic-Tac-Toe!

Test your strategic skills in this classic game of Tic-Tac-Toe. The rules are simple: two players take turns to mark 'X' and 'O' on a 3x3 grid, aiming to be the first to get three of their marks in a row, column, or diagonal.


Players take turns marking empty cells on the grid with their respective symbols: 'X' and 'O'. The objective is to be the first to form a line of three of their symbols either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The game ends when one player achieves this goal or when all the cells on the grid are filled, resulting in a draw.
